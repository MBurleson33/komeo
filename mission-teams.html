Short-Term Missions

Experience the joy of serving in West Africa

Our mission teams serve on short-term trips three times a year — typically in March, July, and November. Each team focuses on orphanage ministry, community outreach, and evangelism to local and remote villages across Sierra Leone.

Trips run 12–14 days and cost approximately $3,200–$3,400. We provide full training, fundraising resources, and support every step of the way.

Before You Apply

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do I need to raise to serve on a team?

+

Trips typically cost $3,200–$3,400 for a 12–14 day trip. The amount depends on team size and the focus of each trip. As you fundraise, we provide training and all the resources you'll need throughout the process.

What is the minimum age requirement?

+

We require team members to be 18 or older. However, we often send a Summer teen/young adult team where the minimum age is 14.

Do I need vaccinations?

+

To acquire a Sierra Leone visa, you must have a Yellow Fever vaccine. We also encourage you to visit the CDC website and consult your doctor for other recommended vaccinations. As an organization, we do not require any vaccinations beyond Yellow Fever for the visa.

Will I need to take medications?

+

We require team members to take an anti-malarial medication. We strongly recommend consulting your doctor well in advance to choose the right option for you.

What will we do on the trip?

+

Every trip includes time at the orphanages loving on and ministering to the children, as well as community outreach and evangelism in local and remote villages. Specific activities vary by trip but always center on building relationships and sharing the Gospel.

How do I apply?

+

Click the "Apply for a Mission Team" button above or below to visit our trip management portal and submit your application. Our team will follow up with next steps.
