Nearly 150 Children

Growing up loved, discipled, and full of hope

Through the faithful giving of sponsors, the Lord has enabled us to care for nearly 150 precious children across two orphanages in Wellington and Waterloo, Sierra Leone.

We are blessed to watch them grow up, learn to trust the Lord, succeed in school, and become all that God created them to be. Many of our sponsored children have gone on to university — some becoming the first women in their families to graduate college.

These are not just recipients of charity. They are future lawyers, doctors, pastors, and leaders of Sierra Leone — and your sponsorship is what makes it possible.

College Sponsorship

Help send a child to university

Currently 43 children from our orphanages are pursuing university — but only about a quarter of them have sponsors committed to covering their college needs. Every semester we take a leap of faith and trust God to fill the gap.

Some of our sponsored college students have become the first women in Sierra Leone's history to graduate with four-year degrees. Joyce was immediately hired as a professor at her university. Rebecca and Tabitha are leading evangelism ministries.

If you'd like to help send a child to university, reach out to us — college sponsorship needs vary by student and we'd love to connect you directly.
