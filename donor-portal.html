Donor Portal

Thank you for partnering with us. Your generosity is making a real difference in Sierra Leone.

Access Your Donor Account

View your personal account information, donation history, and giving statements — all in one place through our secure partner portal.

Access Portal →
First time? If this is your first time accessing the portal, you'll need to create a username and password. Please use the same login each time you visit to ensure no duplicate accounts are created under your name.

What you'll find in the portal

Your personal account information
Complete donation history
Annual giving statements (issued by end of January)
Sponsorship details and child information
Manage recurring giving

Having trouble accessing your account? Contact us at info@komeo.org or call 405-659-6802 and we'll be happy to help.
