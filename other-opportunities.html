Other Opportunities

More ways to make a tangible difference in the lives of children and communities in Sierra Leone.

Special Experience

Beach Trip for the Kids

For many in America, a trip to the beach is a common, fun outing — but for many children in Sierra Leone, it seems like an unattainable dream. Please help us provide a time of fellowship at the beach for all of the children in our orphanages. It's a simple joy that means the world to them.

Give to the Beach Trip

Flexible Giving

General Giving

You can partner with us financially by donating to our general fund. These gifts go where the need is greatest — supporting the daily operations of the orphanages, outreach efforts, and the overall mission of Komeo in Sierra Leone.

Give to the General Fund

In-Kind Donations

Send Items with a Mission Team

Every time a mission team travels to Sierra Leone, they bring supplies for the children and communities we serve. If you'd like to donate items — clothing, school supplies, medical supplies, or other goods — our teams can carry them directly to the orphanages.
