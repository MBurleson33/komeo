Nearly 150 Children

Growing up loved, discipled, and full of hope

Through the faithful giving of sponsors, the Lord has enabled us to care for nearly 150 precious children across two orphanages in Wellington and Waterloo, Sierra Leone.

We are blessed to watch them grow up, learn to trust the Lord, succeed in school, and become all that God created them to be. Many of our sponsored children have gone on to university — some becoming the first women in their families to graduate college.

These are not just recipients of charity. They are future lawyers, doctors, pastors, and leaders of Sierra Leone — and your sponsorship is what makes it possible.

Simple Process

How sponsorship works

1

Browse Children

Visit our sponsorship portal to see children waiting for a sponsor. Filter by age, location, or type.

2

Choose Your Child

Select the child you feel called to sponsor and complete the simple sign-up process through our secure portal.

3

Give Monthly

Your $35/month gift is processed securely. You'll receive a tax receipt and updates about your child.

4

Build a Relationship

Write letters, send gifts through mission teams, and even visit your child in Sierra Leone.

As a Sponsor

More than just a monthly gift

Write Letters

Build a real relationship through letter writing. Many children save these letters for years — they mean the world to them.

Send Gifts

Each year, our mission teams bring gifts to the orphanages. You can include specific items for your child to be hand-delivered.

Pray for Your Child

These children are growing up as Christians in a predominantly Muslim country. Your faithful prayers are powerful and deeply felt.

Visit in Person

Join a mission team and meet your sponsored child face-to-face. The blessing of sponsorship is never more vivid than in that moment.
