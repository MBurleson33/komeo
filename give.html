Ways to Give

Every gift — large or small — goes directly to caring for orphans and sharing the Gospel in Sierra Leone.

Give Online

Secure credit and debit card donations processed through REACHpay. Set up a one-time gift or automatic recurring donations in just a few minutes.

Other Ways to Give

Upon approval of our Board of Directors, we can accept gifts of goods and services, property, vehicles, stocks, or other securities. Many employers also match employee gifts — check with your HR department.

Contact Us
All contributions are tax-deductible to the extent provided by law. Komeo International Ministries is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. For questions about your giving, email info@komeo.org or call 405-659-6802. Annual giving statements are issued by the end of January each year and are accessible through the Donor Portal.
