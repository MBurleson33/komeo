Co-Founders

The Crawfords

Jerome & Shanna Crawford

As co-founders of Komeo, Jerome and Shanna Crawford have been involved since the very beginning. In 2008, Shanna was in Sierra Leone on a short-term trip and saw the immense need for the Gospel there. Soon after, Jerome served on a trip as well.

Since then, God has used them both through over 50 short-term trips — and on long-term missions as they spent nearly 9 months living in Sierra Leone with their 6 children. When you support the Crawfords, your funds cover the cost of the three trips they serve on each year.
