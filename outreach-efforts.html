Outreach Efforts

Meeting physical needs to open doors for the Gospel — in villages across Sierra Leone.

Community Outreach

Beyond the orphanage walls

Through Komeo outreach projects, God has provided for many. We have been blessed with opportunities to supply clean water to remote villages, food to polio and amputee communities, Bibles to prisons, and medical clinics across Sierra Leone. Our hope is that through meeting physical needs, we are able to share the love of Christ and the hope of the Gospel.

Clean Water

We supply clean water to remote villages that lack access to safe drinking water — one of the most fundamental needs in rural Sierra Leone.

Food Distribution

We provide food to polio survivors, amputee communities, and families left vulnerable by Sierra Leone's civil war and the 2014 Ebola outbreak.

Bible Distribution

We bring the Word of God to prisons and remote communities — planting seeds of faith in places where the Gospel has rarely been heard.

Medical Clinics

Our teams run medical clinics in remote villages across Sierra Leone — providing basic healthcare to communities with little or no access to medical services.
