Our Mission

Demonstrating
God's love in
West Africa

Our mission is to share the Gospel of Christ and demonstrate God's love by building relationships and responding compassionately to those with spiritual, physical, and emotional needs.

Through child sponsorship, we provide food, clothing, education, medical care, and — most importantly — Biblical discipleship to nearly 150 orphans across two orphanages in Wellington and Waterloo, Sierra Leone.

Where We Serve

Wellington & Waterloo, Sierra Leone

We serve orphans in local villages devastated by Ebola in 2014 and by a brutal 12-year civil war that ended in 2002. Our community outreach also reaches remote jungle villages including Rokbop, Matsumana, and Mile 91.

Wellington Orphanage

Home to children who lost parents during Sierra Leone's civil war — raised as Christians with a heart to rebuild their nation.

Waterloo Orphanage

A second home where children receive education, discipleship, and the stability of a loving family environment.

Jungle Village Outreach

Bringing Bibles, medical supplies, and the Gospel message to Rokbop, Matsumana, Mile 91, and surrounding remote communities.
