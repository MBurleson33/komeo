Building something that will last generations

Construction is underway on a school and community center for the people of Waterloo, Sierra Leone and surrounding communities. We look forward to using this facility to strengthen the community of Waterloo for decades to come.

Our team of architects, engineers, and builders are working alongside the people of Sierra Leone — utilizing their knowledge, skills, and labor to bring this vision to life. This isn't just a building project. It's an investment in the future of an entire community.

To help us bring this project to completion, we need your prayers and your financial support.

Project Scope

What we're building

Our vision is to expand the primary school currently on the land, adding junior and senior secondary schools that will serve over 1,500 students.
