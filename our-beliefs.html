Our Beliefs

Rooted in Scripture. Centered on the Gospel. United by faith in Jesus Christ.

Statement of Faith

What we believe

Everything Komeo does flows from these convictions — that God loves the world, that the Gospel transforms lives, and that caring for orphans is an act of pure worship.

01

The Word

The Bible is the Word of God — God-breathed and without error. It is the final authority for our faith and living.

Psalm 19:7–8 · 2 Tim 3:16–17 · Heb 4:12
02

The Trinity

God exists eternally as three persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit — one God in perfect unity.

Matt 28:19 · 2 Cor 13:14 · Heb 9:14
03

The Father

The Heavenly Father, in His great love for the world, sent His only begotten Son as the Savior of mankind to provide everlasting life and Fatherhood for every believer.

John 3:16 · Romans 8:14 · 1 John 4:14
04

The Son

Jesus Christ is the eternal Son of God — born of a virgin, crucified for our sins, risen bodily from the grave, and returning again for His people.

John 1:1,14 · 1 Cor 15:3–9 · Acts 1:9–11
05

The Holy Spirit

The Holy Spirit convicts us of sin, leads us to confess Jesus as Lord, indwells us to illuminate Scripture, and empowers us to live godly lives.

John 16:7–14 · Rom 8:9 · 1 Cor 12:13
06

Sin & Redemption

Every person is a sinner and separated from God until repenting and receiving God's gift of eternal life by trusting in Jesus Christ alone as Savior and Lord.

Rom 3:23 · Rom 6:23 · Eph 2:8–9
07

Salvation

Believers are eternally safe and secure in Jesus, and give evidence of genuine faith through a life of good works and love for others.

John 3:16 · John 10:27–29 · James 2:14–17
08

The Church

The church is the living body of Christ on earth — comprised of all believers, devoted to worshipping God, maturing disciples, and reaching the lost with the Gospel.

Matt 28:19–20 · Eph 1:22–23 · Heb 10:25
09

The Choice

Within God's ultimate sovereignty, each person has the genuine freedom to accept or reject His offer of salvation through Jesus Christ.

John 3:16 · Rev 22:17 · Rom 10:9–10
"For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son, that whoever believes in him should not perish but have eternal life."
John 3:16

Compelled by the Gospel.

These beliefs drive everything we do in Sierra Leone. Join us in living them out through sponsorship, mission, and giving.
